About

Have you ever had trouble making plans with friends? Do you know what activity you would want to do, but have trouble finding a convenient location?
Our Coffee Time allows you to easily meet up with your friends by providing a list of hangout spots close to all of you.
All kinds of places are provided, whether you're looking to taste new flavors as you dine at a local restaurant, feel the sun on your skin as you trek through the great outdoors,
experience something new with some courageous companions, or just enjoy a drink with the laughter of your closest chums in the background.

To use Our Coffee Time, simply head over to the "Plan" tab, select all the activities you plan to do with your friends, and click "Submit."
Input your address and those of your friends, and there will be a list of convenient hangout spots for all of you.
